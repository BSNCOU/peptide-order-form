## Administration Route Reference

### Subcutaneous (SC) Only
- Semaglutide
- Tirzepatide
- Retatrutide
- BPC-157
- Most peptides default to SC

### Intramuscular (IM) Preferred
- MOTS-c (better absorption)
- NAD+ (some prefer for comfort)
- TB-500 (either SC or IM)
- Glutathione

### Intranasal Option
- Semax (preferred route)
- Selank (preferred route)

- BPC-157 (for some applications)

### Multiple Routes
- BPC-157 (SC, IM, oral, intranasal)
- NAD+ (SC, IM, IV in clinical setting)
- Glutathione (SC, IM, IV)

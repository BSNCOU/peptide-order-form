## BPC-157

### Overview
BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound) is a synthetic peptide derived from a protective protein found in gastric juice. It has powerful healing and regenerative properties.

### Available Specifications
- **BC5**: 5mg/10vials
- **BC10**: 10mg/10vials

### Reconstitution
- Reconstitute with 2ml BAC water
- 5mg vial = 2.5mg/ml
- 10mg vial = 5mg/ml

### Dosage Protocol

**Systemic Healing:**
- 250-500mcg per day
- Single dose or split into 2 doses
- Cycle: 4-8 weeks, then 2-4 weeks off

**Localized Injury:**
- 250-500mcg per day
- Inject as close to injury site as comfortable
- OR systemic (any location) also effective

**Gut Health:**
- 250-500mcg per day
- Preferably on empty stomach
- Can be taken orally or by injection

### Administration

**Injection Sites:**
- **For localized injury**: Within 2-3 inches of affected area
- **For systemic effects**: Subcutaneous anywhere (abdomen common)
- **For gut issues**: Oral or subcutaneous

**Timing:**
- Morning (fasted) OR
- Before bed
- Consistent timing recommended

### Cycle Recommendations

**Acute Injury:**
- 4-6 weeks continuous use
- Re-evaluate at 4 weeks

**Chronic Issues:**
- 8-12 weeks
- 4-week break
- Can repeat cycles

**Gut Health:**
- 4-8 weeks initially
- Maintenance: 2-3x weekly long-term

### Expected Outcomes

**Injury Healing:**
- Accelerated tendon healing
- Faster ligament repair
- Improved muscle recovery
- Bone healing enhancement
- Reduced inflammation

**Gut Health:**
- Healed intestinal lining
- Reduced inflammation
- Improved digestion
- Better nutrient absorption

**Other Benefits:**
- Neuroprotective effects
- Improved blood flow
- Reduced pain
- Enhanced collagen production

### Specific Applications

**Tendonitis/Tendinopathy:**
- 500mcg daily
- Inject near affected tendon
- 4-6 weeks minimum

**Muscle Tears:**
- 500mcg daily
- Site-specific or systemic
- 4-8 weeks

**Joint Issues:**
- 250-500mcg daily
- Can combine with TB-500
- 6-12 weeks

**Gut Issues (IBS, IBD, Leaky Gut):**
- 250-500mcg daily
- Oral or injection
- 8-12 weeks

### Best Practices
- Stay hydrated
- Adequate protein intake
- Proper rest and recovery
- Physical therapy if applicable
- Avoid NSAIDs if possible (may interfere)

### Safety Notes
- Very well tolerated
- Minimal side effects
- Can be used long-term with cycling
- No known negative drug interactions

## TB-500

### Overview
TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4) is a peptide that promotes healing, reduces inflammation, and supports tissue repair throughout the body.

### Available Specifications
- **TB5**: 5mg/10vials
- **TB10**: 10mg/10vials

### Reconstitution
- Reconstitute with 2ml BAC water

### Dosage Protocol

**Loading Phase (First 4-6 weeks):**
- 5-10mg per week
- Split into 2-3 injections
- Example: 2.5mg twice weekly

**Maintenance Phase:**
- 2-5mg per week
- Once or twice weekly
- Example: 2mg once weekly

**Acute Injury:**
- Higher end of dosing (10mg/week)
- Loading phase for 4-6 weeks
- Then maintenance

### Administration
- Subcutaneous or intramuscular
- Systemic action (doesn't need to be near injury)
- Any body location
- Rotate injection sites

### Cycle Recommendations

**Injury Recovery:**
- 6-8 weeks loading
- 4-8 weeks maintenance
- Can extend based on progress

**General Health/Prevention:**
- 4 weeks on
- 2-4 weeks off
- Repeat as needed

**Chronic Issues:**
- 12+ weeks
- Ongoing maintenance possible

### Expected Outcomes

**Healing Benefits:**
- Enhanced wound healing
- Faster recovery from injury
- Improved flexibility
- Reduced scar tissue
- Hair growth (in some cases)

**Systemic Effects:**
- Reduced inflammation throughout body
- Better cardiovascular health
- Neuroprotective benefits
- Enhanced immune function
- Improved stem cell migration

**Athletic Performance:**
- Faster recovery between workouts
- Improved endurance
- Better injury resilience
- Enhanced flexibility and range of motion

### Specific Applications

**Major Injury Recovery:**
- Loading: 5mg twice weekly for 6 weeks
- Maintenance: 2mg weekly for 6-12 weeks

**Chronic Inflammation:**
- 2-5mg weekly ongoing
- Monitor inflammatory markers

**Athletic Performance:**
- 2-5mg weekly during heavy training
- Cycle off during deload weeks

**Hair Growth:**
- 2-5mg weekly
- Minimum 12 weeks
- Results vary

### Synergies
- Excellent with BPC-157
- Combines well with GHK-Cu
- Enhanced by proper nutrition
- Supported by adequate sleep

### Side Effects
- Generally very well tolerated
- Possible temporary fatigue
- Mild headache (rare)
- Injection site reaction (minor)

## BPC-157 + TB-500 Combination

### Overview

### Available Specifications

### Reconstitution
- Reconstitute with 2ml BAC water

### Dosage Protocol

**Standard Healing Protocol:**
- BPC-157: 250-500mcg daily
- TB-500: 2.5-5mg twice weekly

**Using Pre-Mixed Blend:**
- Follow product concentration
- Typical: 1 injection daily (BPC component) + 2-3 weekly (TB component)

**Acute Injury Protocol:**
- Higher doses of both
- BPC: 500mcg daily
- TB: 5mg twice weekly
- 4-6 weeks loading

### Administration Strategy

**Option 1 - Separate Timing:**
- BPC-157: Daily (morning or evening)
- TB-500: 2-3x weekly (can be different days)

**Option 2 - Combined:**
- Use pre-mixed blend
- Follow recommended protocol
- Simplifies administration

### Cycle Protocol

**Week 1-6: Loading Phase**
- BPC-157: 500mcg daily
- TB-500: 5mg twice weekly
- Aggressive healing approach

**Week 7-12: Maintenance**
- BPC-157: 250mcg daily
- TB-500: 2mg once weekly
- Sustained recovery

**Week 13+: As Needed**
- Reduce to 2-3x weekly (BPC)

**Systemic Improvements:**

**Performance:**

- Improved tissue quality
- Better range of motion

### Best Applications

**Major Injury Recovery:**

- Post-surgical healing
- Chronic pain conditions
- Joint issues

**Athletic Enhancement:**
- Heavy training periods
- Post-competition recovery
- Injury prevention
- Longevity in sport

**General Health:**
- Gut healing
- Inflammation reduction
- Aging gracefully
- Quality of life improvement

### Monitoring Progress

**Injury Healing:**
- 2-3mg per day
- Daily until healed

**Cosmetic/Skin:**
- 1mg per day subcutaneous
- Can also apply topically

### Administration

**Injection:**
- Subcutaneous preferred
- Facial injections for cosmetic use (by trained professional)

**Topical:**

- 12 weeks on
- 4 weeks off
- Long-term cycling

**Healing:**
- Continuous until resolution
- Then maintain 2-3x weekly

**Cosmetic:**
- Ongoing use acceptable
- Best results with consistent use

### Expected Outcomes

**Skin Benefits:**
- Improved skin elasticity
- Reduced wrinkles and fine lines
- Enhanced collagen production
- Better skin firmness
- Improved skin tone and texture
- Scar reduction

**Healing:**
- Faster wound healing
- Improved tissue repair

- Better hair growth
- Nail strengthening

**Systemic:**
- Anti-inflammatory effects
- Antioxidant properties
- Improved immune function

## General Guidelines for Tissue Repair Peptides

### Optimal Healing Environment

**Nutrition:**
- High protein (2g+ per kg)
- Adequate vitamin C (1000-2000mg daily)
- Collagen supplementation
- Omega-3 fatty acids
- Zinc and copper adequate

**Lifestyle:**
- Quality sleep (8+ hours)
- Stress management
- Avoid smoking/alcohol
- Stay hydrated
- Gentle movement (as appropriate)

### Monitoring Recovery

**Objective Measures:**
- Range of motion tests
- Strength assessments
- Pain scale tracking
- Functional movement tests
- Imaging when appropriate

**Subjective Measures:**
- Daily pain logs
- Activity tolerance
- Quality of life assessments
- Sleep quality
- Overall well-being

### When to Seek Medical Attention
- No improvement after 4 weeks
- Worsening symptoms
- Severe pain
- Signs of infection
- Unusual side effects

### Combining Healing Peptides

**Synergistic Combinations:**
- BPC-157 + TB-500 (most popular)
- BPC-157 + GHK-Cu (for skin/wounds)
- TB-500 + GHK-Cu (systemic healing)
- All three for comprehensive healing

**Timing:**
- Can all be taken same day
- Use different injection sites
- Spread throughout day if possible

## Semax

### Overview
Semax is a synthetic peptide analog of ACTH that enhances cognitive function, provides neuroprotection, and improves focus and mental clarity.

### Available Specifications
- **XA5**: 5mg/10vials
- **XA11**: 11mg/10vials

### Reconstitution
- Reconstitute with 2ml BAC water
- Can also use sterile saline for nasal administration

### Dosage Protocol

**Cognitive Enhancement:**
- 250-500mcg per day
- Split into 1-2 doses
- Cycle: 4-8 weeks on, 2-4 weeks off

**Therapeutic (Neuroprotection):**
- 300-600mcg per day
- 2-3 doses daily
- 8-12 weeks

**Performance (Exams, Intensive Work):**
- 500-1000mcg per day
- During high-demand periods
- 2-4 weeks

### Administration Methods

**Intranasal (Most Common):**
- 150-300mcg per nostril
- 1-3 times daily
- Best absorption method
- Effects within 5-15 minutes

**Subcutaneous:**
- 200-500mcg per injection
- Once or twice daily
- Longer-lasting effects

**Sublingual:**
- 250-500mcg
- Hold under tongue 2-3 minutes
- 1-2 times daily

### Timing

**For Cognitive Performance:**
- Morning upon waking
- Mid-morning for sustained focus
- Avoid late afternoon (may affect sleep)

**For Learning:**
- Before study sessions
- During information acquisition
- Enhances memory consolidation

### Cycle Recommendations

**Standard:**
- 4-6 weeks on
- 2 weeks off
- Prevents tolerance

**Intensive (Short-term):**
- 2-3 weeks daily
- For specific demanding periods
- Longer break after (3-4 weeks)

**Long-term Neuroprotection:**
- 5 days on, 2 days off
- Ongoing with periodic breaks
- Medical supervision recommended

### Expected Outcomes

**Cognitive:**
- Enhanced focus and concentration
- Improved memory encoding and recall
- Better mental clarity
- Faster information processing
- Enhanced learning capacity
- Improved pattern recognition

**Mental Performance:**
- Increased productivity
- Better stress tolerance
- Enhanced creativity
- Improved problem-solving
- Mental stamina

**Neuroprotective:**
- Protection from oxidative stress
- Enhanced neuroplasticity
- Improved brain blood flow
- Better oxygen utilization
- BDNF increase

**Mood & Motivation:**
- Improved mood
- Reduced anxiety (mild)
- Enhanced motivation
- Better stress response
- Increased mental energy

### Specific Applications

**Academic Performance:**
- 300-500mcg before study sessions
- Enhances learning and retention
- Improves exam performance

**Professional Demands:**
- Daily during high-stress periods
- Maintains mental clarity
- Reduces cognitive fatigue

**Recovery from Brain Injury:**
- 300-600mcg daily
- Neuroprotective effects
- Supports recovery
- Medical supervision essential

**Athletic Performance:**
- Enhanced focus during competition
- Better mind-muscle connection
- Improved reaction time

### Side Effects
- Generally very well tolerated
- Occasional headache (mild)
- Rarely: restlessness or overstimulation
- No significant withdrawal

### Best Practices
- Start with lower doses
- Take early in day
- Stay hydrated
- Adequate sleep important
- Combine with good nutrition
- B-vitamin support beneficial

### Synergies
- Excellent with Selank
- Works well with racetams (if used)
- Enhanced by omega-3s
- Supported by quality sleep
- Combines with other nootropics

## VIP (Vasoactive Intestinal Peptide)

### Overview
VIP is a neuropeptide with anti-inflammatory, neuroprotective, and immune-modulating properties, particularly useful for chronic inflammatory conditions and mold toxicity.

### Available Specifications
- **VIP5**: 5mg/10vials
- **VIP10**: 10mg/10vials

### Reconstitution
- Reconstitute with 2ml sterile saline (preferred for nasal)
- BAC water acceptable

### Dosage Protocol

**Mold Toxicity/CIRS:**
- 50mcg 4 times daily
- Intranasal
- 3-6 months
- Under medical supervision

**General Inflammation:**
- 50-100mcg 2-3 times daily
- 8-12 weeks
- Monitor response

**Neuroprotection:**
- 50mcg 2-4 times daily
- As needed or cycled

### Administration
- **Intranasal only** for most protocols
- Use nasal spray bottle
- Alternate nostrils
- 4 times daily spacing (morning, midday, afternoon, evening)

### Cycle Recommendations
- Typically long-term use (months)
- Medical supervision essential
- Monitor biomarkers
- Adjust based on response

### Expected Outcomes

**CIRS/Mold Toxicity:**
- Reduced inflammation
- Improved energy
- Better cognitive function
- Reduced brain fog
- Normalized immune response

**General Health:**
- Anti-inflammatory effects
- Neuroprotection
- Improved gut health
- Better immune regulation
- Enhanced tissue repair

**Respiratory:**
- Bronchodilation
- Reduced airway inflammation
- Improved breathing
- Protective for lungs

### Special Considerations
- Often used in specific medical protocols
- Shoemaker Protocol for CIRS
- Requires proper diagnosis
- Medical supervision recommended
- Monitor specific biomarkers

### Monitoring
- C4a levels (CIRS)
- TGF-beta1 (CIRS)
- MMP-9 (inflammation)
- Symptom tracking
- Cognitive function assessments

## DSIP (Delta Sleep-Inducing Peptide)

### Overview
DSIP promotes deep, restorative sleep and has stress-protective and neuromodulatory effects.

### Available Specifications
- **DS5**: 5mg/10vials
- **DS15**: 15mg/10vials (actually DS10 based on product list)

### Reconstitution
- Reconstitute with 2ml BAC water

### Dosage Protocol

**Sleep Enhancement:**
- 100-300mcg per night
- 30-60 minutes before bed
- Daily or as needed

**Stress Management:**
- 100-200mcg daily
- Can be taken evening or split doses

**Intensive Sleep Repair:**
- 300-500mcg per night
- 2-4 weeks
- For severe sleep issues

### Administration
- Subcutaneous or intramuscular
- Preferably evening/bedtime
- Can be daytime for stress (lower dose)

### Timing
- 30-60 minutes before desired sleep time
- Consistent timing optimal
- Can be used as needed

### Expected Outcomes

**Sleep Quality:**
- Deeper sleep
- More restorative sleep
- Increased slow-wave sleep
- Better sleep architecture
- Wake refreshed

**Stress & Recovery:**
- Better stress adaptation
- Improved HPA axis function
- Enhanced recovery
- Reduced anxiety
- Normalized cortisol patterns

**Cognitive:**
- Improved daytime alertness (from better sleep)
- Better memory consolidation
- Enhanced mood
- Reduced brain fog

**Physical:**
- Better muscle recovery
- Improved growth hormone release during sleep
- Enhanced immune function

### Best For
- Sleep disorders
- Stress-related sleep issues
- Shift work
- Travel (jet lag)
- Recovery optimization
- Stress management

### Side Effects
- Very well tolerated
- May cause drowsiness (desired effect)
- Vivid dreams (occasionally)
- Morning grogginess (rare, dose-dependent)

### Best Practices
- Take on empty stomach for best results
- Establish good sleep hygiene
- Consistent sleep schedule
- Dark, cool bedroom
- Avoid screens before bed

### Synergies
- Works with melatonin (careful with timing)
- Enhanced by magnesium
- Combines with sleep optimization
- Supported by relaxation techniques

## General Guidelines for Cognitive Peptides

### Optimizing Cognitive Enhancement

**Foundational Support:**
- Quality sleep (7-9 hours)
- Balanced nutrition
- Regular exercise
- Stress management
- Hydration
- B-vitamins, omega-3s

**Lifestyle Factors:**
- Mental stimulation
- Learning new skills
- Social engagement
- Physical activity
- Stress reduction
- Avoid neurotoxins (alcohol, smoking)

### Stacking Cognitive Peptides

**The Focus Stack:**
- Semax: 250-300mcg morning
- Purpose: Enhanced focus and learning

**The Calm Focus Stack:**
- Semax: 250mcg morning
- Selank: 250mcg morning
- Purpose: Focused yet calm mental state

**The Complete Cognitive Stack:**
- Semax: 300mcg morning
- Selank: 250mcg afternoon
- DSIP: 200mcg before bed
- Purpose: All-day cognitive enhancement plus recovery

**The Neuroprotection Stack:**
- Semax: 300mcg daily
- Pinealon: Cycles
- Cerebrolysin: 2-3x weekly
- Purpose: Long-term brain health

### Timing Strategies

**Morning Protocol:**
- Semax for focus
- Begin mental work 15-30 minutes after

**Throughout Day:**
- Morning: Stimulating peptides (Semax)
- Afternoon: Calming if needed (Selank)
- Evening: Sleep support (DSIP)

**Before Mental Performance:**
- 30-60 minutes prior
- Empty stomach preferred
- Stay hydrated

### Monitoring Cognitive Enhancement

**Subjective Tracking:**
- Daily focus ratings
- Productivity logs
- Mood tracking
- Energy levels
- Sleep quality

**Objective Measures:**
- Cognitive assessments
- Memory tests
- Reaction time
- Work output
- Error rates

### Safety Considerations

**General Safety:**
- Start low, go slow
- One new peptide at a time
- Monitor for side effects
- Take breaks (prevent tolerance)
- Medical supervision for therapeutic use

**Contraindications:**
- Pregnancy/breastfeeding
- Active psychosis
- Severe anxiety (start carefully with stimulating peptides)
- Uncontrolled medical conditions

**Drug Interactions:**
- Generally minimal
- Caution with stimulants
- Consider with anxiety medications
- Discuss with healthcare provider

### When to Use Cognitive Peptides

**Appropriate Situations:**
- Demanding work periods
- Academic requirements
- Learning new skills
- Recovery from brain injury
- Age-related cognitive decline
- Performance enhancement

**Not Appropriate:**
- Replacing sleep or nutrition
- Avoiding necessary rest
- Masking medical conditions
- Long-term without breaks
- Without lifestyle optimization

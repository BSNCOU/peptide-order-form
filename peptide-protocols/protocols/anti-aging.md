## Epithalon

### Overview
Epithalon (Epitalon) is a synthetic peptide that activates telomerase enzyme, potentially extending cellular lifespan and promoting longevity.

### Available Specifications
- **ET10**: 10mg/10vials
- **ET50**: 50mg/10vials

### Reconstitution
- Reconstitute with 2ml BAC water

### Dosage Protocol

**Standard Cycle:**
- 5-10mg total per cycle
- Split over 10-20 days
- Example: 500mcg daily for 10 days OR 250mcg daily for 20 days

**Intensive Cycle:**
- 10-20mg total per cycle
- 1mg daily for 10-20 days
- For more pronounced effects

**Frequency:**
- 1-2 cycles per year
- Spring and Fall commonly chosen
- Some use 3-4 cycles per year

### Administration
- Subcutaneous injection
- Best taken in evening before bed
- Can be taken any time consistently

### Cycle Examples

**Conservative Protocol:**
- Day 1-10: 500mcg daily (5mg total)
- Wait 6 months
- Repeat

**Standard Protocol:**
- Day 1-10: 1mg daily (10mg total)
- Wait 4-6 months
- Repeat

**Intensive Protocol:**
- Day 1-20: 1mg daily (20mg total)
- Wait 3-4 months
- Repeat

### Expected Outcomes

**Cellular Level:**
- Telomerase activation
- Telomere lengthening
- Enhanced DNA repair
- Improved cellular function

**Systemic Effects:**
- Better sleep quality and regulation
- Improved melatonin production
- Enhanced circadian rhythm
- Normalized hormone levels
- Improved skin elasticity

**Long-term Benefits:**
- Potential lifespan extension
- Reduced biological aging markers
- Improved immune function
- Better overall health span

**Age-Related Improvements:**
- Enhanced vision
- Improved memory
- Better skin appearance
- Increased energy levels
- Normalized blood pressure

### Best Practices
- Use in cycles (not continuous)
- Track subjective improvements
- Combine with healthy lifestyle
- Consider biomarker testing
- Quality sleep during cycle

### Monitoring
- Sleep quality logs
- Energy level tracking
- Skin appearance (photos)
- Telomere testing (optional, expensive)
- Overall health markers

## NAD+

### Overview
NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a critical coenzyme involved in energy production, DNA repair, and cellular health.

### Available Specifications
- **NJ100**: 100mg/10vials
- **NJ500**: 500mg/10vials

### Reconstitution
- Reconstitute with 2ml sterile water or BAC water
- Use larger volume (5-10ml) for more comfortable injection

### Dosage Protocol

**Maintenance Dose:**
- 50-100mg per session
- 1-3 times per week

**Therapeutic Dose:**
- 100-250mg per session
- 2-3 times per week

**Intensive Protocol:**
- 250-500mg per session
- 2-3 times per week
- Short-term (4-8 weeks)

### Administration

**Subcutaneous:**
- Slower injection better tolerated
- Can cause burning sensation
- Dilute more for comfort

**Intramuscular:**
- Better tolerated by some
- Inject slowly over 5-10 minutes
- Larger muscle groups

**Intravenous (Clinical Setting):**
- Most effective but requires medical supervision
- 250-1000mg per session
- Slow drip over 2-4 hours

### Injection Technique for Comfort
- Use larger dilution volume
- Inject very slowly (5-10 minutes)
- Ice area before injection
- Massage area after
- Start with lower doses

### Cycle Recommendations

**Beginner:**
- 50mg twice weekly
- 8-12 weeks
- Assess response

**Intermediate:**
- 100-150mg 2-3x weekly
- 8-12 weeks
- 2-4 weeks break

**Advanced:**
- 200-250mg 3x weekly
- 4-8 weeks intensive
- 4 weeks maintenance
- Cycle as needed

### Expected Outcomes

**Energy & Metabolism:**
- Increased cellular energy (ATP)
- Enhanced mitochondrial function
- Improved metabolism
- Reduced fatigue

**Cognitive:**
- Better mental clarity
- Improved focus and concentration
- Enhanced memory
- Neuroprotection

**Anti-Aging:**
- DNA repair enhancement
- Improved skin appearance
- Better cellular function
- Reduced oxidative stress

**Recovery:**
- Faster recovery from exercise
- Reduced muscle soreness
- Better athletic performance
- Enhanced endurance

**Addiction Recovery:**
- Reduced cravings
- Improved mood
- Better brain function during recovery
- Support for detoxification

### Side Effects
- Burning/stinging at injection site (common)
- Temporary flushing
- Mild nausea (rare)
- Headache (usually if dehydrated)

### Managing Discomfort
- Increase dilution volume
- Inject more slowly
- Use ice before/after
- Start with lower doses
- Consider IM instead of SQ

### Best Practices
- Stay well hydrated
- Take with B-complex vitamins
- Avoid alcohol during intensive protocols
- Adequate sleep
- Quality nutrition

### Synergies
- Excellent with MOTS-c
- Works well with CoQ10
- Enhanced by resveratrol
- Supported by B vitamins
- Combines with other longevity protocols
